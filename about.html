About Us

Brief History

Kings College Budo, founded in 1906, is one of Uganda's most prestigious schools. Known for its rich history and academic excellence, the school has nurtured generations of leaders and professionals.

Mission

To provide a holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and leadership skills in a dynamic and inclusive environment.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, producing globally competitive and socially responsible individuals.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Inclusivity
  • Leadership
  • Service to the community
