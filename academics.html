Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects are offered at King's College Budo?

We offer a comprehensive curriculum including:

  • Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
  • Humanities: History, Geography, Literature, Religious Studies
  • Technical subjects: Computer Science, Fine Art, Music
  • Languages: English, Kiswahili
What are the class sizes at King's College Budo?

We maintain an average class size of 30-35 students to ensure personalized attention and optimal learning conditions.

Are there extra-curricular activities available?

Yes, we offer numerous activities including:

  • Sports: Football, Basketball, Cricket, Athletics
  • Clubs: Debate Club, Science Club, Music Club
  • Leadership opportunities through prefectship
What are the academic terms and hours?

The academic year is divided into three terms. School hours are from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday through Friday.

How can parents track their child's progress?

We provide:

  • Regular progress reports
  • Parent-teacher meetings each term
  • Online portal access for continuous monitoring
  • Direct communication with teachers through official channels
